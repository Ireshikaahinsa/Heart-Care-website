Giving

Your donation helps save lives



It's distressing but true: Someone dies from heart disease, stroke or another cardiovascular disease every 43 seconds in this country.

When you donate to the Heart care Association, you are joining us in the fight against our nation’s No. 1 and No. 4 killers – heart disease and stroke.

Your contribution will support such life-saving efforts as research, education, advocating for better health, improving patient care and reaching the millions of people at risk. Your generosity brings us one step closer to a world free of heart disease and stroke.

In short, you’re helping to save lives.

Honor a loved one

A memorial or tribute is a meaningful way to celebrate someone.

If you've lost someone you care deeply about, a fundraiser is a beautiful way to honor them and help save the lives of others.

Every rupee you raise helps people in your community live longer, healthier lives and honors those who have touched your life.

You are not alone. Join the fight against heart disease and stroke and start a fundraiser in memory today.


Help save lives all year long

Become a monthly supporter today.

We all want to enjoy more of life's precious moments and experience the wonder we hope each tomorrow holds, but creating a world free of heart disease and stroke requires ongoing dedication and commitment. Please give a monthly gift and support the year-round fight to save more lives.

Volunteering

Your valuable time and effort are essential to helping fight heart disease and stroke



Volunteer your time

Find volunteer opportunities that fit your time and talent.

  • Hands-Only CPR

    Become a lifesaver. Watch our one-minute Hands-Only CPR video to learn to save a life, and share the link with family and friends.

  • Health eHeart Study

    Join the ground breaking Health eHeart study to help improve our understanding of heart disease.

  • Host Your Own Fundraiser

    Host your own online fundraising event to make it easy and fun to donate to help save lives from heart disease and stroke

  • Social Media Volunteer

    Follow @heart_care_association on Facebook, Twitter and Instagram and share our life-saving messages with your networks

  • Support Network

    Give encouragement and tips to heart and stroke patients, families and caretakers via our online forum, or lead an online or offline support group.
