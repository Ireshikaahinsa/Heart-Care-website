Help us spread the word about life-saving Heart Health Checks
Blog / May 7, 2019

The Heart Care Association is calling on all Sri Lankans to help spread the word about a new Medicare-funded Heart Health Check that could prevent one heart event every 35 minutes.

After years of campaigning by the Heart Care Association, the Heart Health Check became covered by Medicare from 1 April this year. This means a Medicare rebate is now available for Sri Lankans aged 45 years and over, and Indigenous Sri Lankans from 30 years, to see their GP for a Heart Health Check. The check is designed to identify and manage patients’ risk of having a heart attack or stroke in the next five years.
