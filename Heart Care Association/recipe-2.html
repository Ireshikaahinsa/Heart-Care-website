Healthy Recipe

Avocado caprese salad

Method

1. Spread rocket over the bottom of a serving platter. Overlap the tomato, avocado and mozzarella on top of the rocket.

2. Scatter over basil leaves, then drizzle with olive oil and a sprinkle of pepper.

Ingredients

2 cups rocket
1½ tomatoes, thickly sliced
½ avocado, cut into thin sliced
¼ reduced fat mozzarella or bocconcini ball (roughly 100g), thinly sliced
4 tablespoons basil leaves, roughly chopped
1 tablespoon olive oil
